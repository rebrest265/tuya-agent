Documentation & Overview

This collector agent runs in the background to scrape environmental metrics from Tuya sensors. The polled metrics are parsed dynamically based on the device product signature, cached locally, and then written into InfluxDB.

Supported Sensor Frameworks:

  • T & H Sensor (Type 1): Parses temp_current (divided by 10), humidity_value, and battery_state (string).
  • Temperature & Humidity Sensor (Type 2): Parses va_temperature (divided by 10), va_humidity, and battery_percentage (integer).
